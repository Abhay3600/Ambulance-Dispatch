# Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Addressed and Project Alignment

## Project Title
**Intelligent Ambulance Dispatch and Route Optimization System**

---

## Introduction

The Intelligent Ambulance Dispatch and Route Optimization project aims to reduce emergency response time by selecting the nearest available ambulance and computing the fastest route using efficient graph algorithms. The system leverages algorithmic optimization to improve decision-making in emergency healthcare services.  

This project directly contributes to multiple **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)** by improving healthcare accessibility, urban efficiency, and public safety through technology-driven solutions.

---

## SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

### SDG Objective
Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

### Alignment with the Project

Emergency medical response time plays a critical role in saving lives. Delays in ambulance arrival can significantly increase mortality rates during accidents, cardiac arrests, and other medical emergencies.

This project directly supports **SDG 3** by:
- Reducing ambulance response time using shortest-path algorithms.
- Ensuring faster access to emergency medical care.
- Improving survival chances by optimizing ambulance routing decisions.
- Supporting healthcare systems with intelligent decision-making tools.

By minimizing travel time and selecting the best ambulance, the system enhances the effectiveness of emergency healthcare services, thereby promoting better health outcomes.
